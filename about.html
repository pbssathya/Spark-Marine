About Spark Marine Services

Founded in 2008 in Dubai, Spark Marine Services (L.L.C) has grown from a single tugboat into a diversified marine services provider with offices across the Gulf, Asia and West Africa. Our mission is to deliver world-class maritime solutions that protect people, assets and the environment.

Core Values

  • Safety First – zero incidents culture.
  • Integrity – transparency in every contract.
  • Innovation – embracing new tech & green fuels.
